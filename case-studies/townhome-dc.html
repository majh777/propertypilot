The Challenge: Complex Multi-Property Management

Capitol Hill Townhomes, a 124-unit portfolio of historic townhomes in Washington DC's prestigious Capitol Hill neighborhood, faced mounting operational challenges and declining profitability.

Critical Issues:

  • 83% average occupancy despite prime location near Capitol and Supreme Court
  • $847K annual maintenance costs for aging historic properties
  • Inconsistent rent pricing across similar units in the portfolio
  • High tenant turnover (31% annually) due to maintenance delays
  • Administrative burden managing 124 individual properties scattered across 8 blocks

Managing historic townhomes required specialized expertise while competing with modern apartment complexes offering consistent amenities and responsive management. The portfolio needed integrated solutions to optimize operations while preserving historic character.
