The Challenge: Rising Costs, Complex Compliance

Desert Medical Plaza, a 180,000 square foot medical office building in Phoenix, faced mounting operational challenges as healthcare regulations intensified and energy costs soared in Arizona's extreme climate.

Critical Issues:

  • $2.47M annual operating costs with 23% increases over 3 years
  • Complex HVAC requirements for specialized medical equipment and patient comfort
  • Stringent compliance requirements for air quality, temperature control, and emergency systems
  • High tenant turnover (28% annually) due to operational inefficiencies
  • Manual building management consuming 40 hours weekly of staff time

The building housed 47 medical practices ranging from general practitioners to specialized surgical centers, each with unique environmental requirements. Traditional building management couldn't accommodate the diverse needs while maintaining operational efficiency.
