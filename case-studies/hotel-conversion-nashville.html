Hotel Conversion Challenge

Music City Developers acquired a struggling 180-room downtown Nashville hotel for conversion to luxury apartments. The project required complex analysis of optimal unit mix, renovation scope, and market positioning to ensure successful transformation.

AI-Driven Conversion Strategy
