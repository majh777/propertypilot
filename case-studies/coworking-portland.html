The Challenge: Underutilized Premium Space

Innovation Hub Portland, a 32,000 square foot coworking space in the Pearl District, was struggling with inefficient space utilization despite strong membership demand in Portland's thriving tech scene.

Critical Pain Points:

  • 47% average space utilization with frequent overcrowding in popular areas
  • Member complaints about unavailable workspace during peak hours
  • Underutilized premium meeting rooms and private offices
  • No data-driven insights into member behavior patterns
  • Manual space management consuming 25 hours weekly of staff time

Despite charging $350/month for dedicated desks and $180 for hot desks, Innovation Hub was leaving revenue on the table while members experienced frustration with space availability. The disconnect between supply and demand was hurting member satisfaction and limiting growth potential.
