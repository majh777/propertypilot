The Strip Center Struggle

Desert Vista Shopping Centers manages 12 neighborhood strip centers across Phoenix metro, totaling 480,000 square feet of retail space. The portfolio faced mounting challenges from e-commerce competition, changing consumer habits, and poor tenant mix decisions that created a downward spiral of vacancy and declining revenues.
