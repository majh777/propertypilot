Not all leads are created equal. Top agents use AI to identify and prioritize the highest-value prospects, ensuring they spend their time on leads most likely to convert.

Advanced AI systems analyze dozens of factors to score leads:

  • Timeline Indicators: Mortgage pre-qualification, current lease expiration
  • Communication Quality: Response speed, question specificity, phone number provided
  • Market Conditions: Inventory levels, seasonal trends, interest rates
