ROI Calculation Framework

Social Media Marketing ROI Formula

ROI = (Social Media Revenue - Social Media Investment) / Social Media Investment × 100

Sample ROI Calculation:

  • Monthly Investment: $2,000 (ads + tools + time)
  • Leads Generated: 45 qualified leads
  • Conversion Rate: 15% (7 new clients)
  • Average Commission: $8,000 per transaction
  • Monthly Revenue: $56,000 (7 × $8,000)
  • ROI: ($56,000 - $2,000) / $2,000 × 100 = 2,700%
